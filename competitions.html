10 km race in Dnipro

My second running adventure. This time 10 km race on Almaz Group Dnipro Marathon. I improved my personal record by 4 minutes at once - 48:48 on the chip.

New emotions, new impressions, new discoveries. Even a year ago, I would hardly have run a couple of kilometers with an average heart rate of 179. But here I managed to do it for as much as 10 km. Running very much educates mentally, disciplines, teaches you do not to compare yourself with others, but teaches you to compare yourself with yesterday's self. And when you get stronger, today's involuntarily slips out of your mouth: "Well done!"
