Asics GEL-Nimbus 22 Review

Asics GEL-Nimbus 22 is my favourite shoe, focusing on softness, more gel technology for extra cushioning and Flytefoam to help propel under the heel.

I would recommend this shoe for the following type of runner:

  • Neutral runner
  • Someone who wants to rack up the miles
  • Someone who likes a lot of cushioning
  • Likes a super comfy feel
  • Likes a responsive feel under foot
